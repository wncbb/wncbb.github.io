BFS

2020-01-19 17:41:20

WHITE: before join the queue GRAY: in the queue BLACK: pop from queue, and after visit all the children

BFS(G, s)
    for each u in (G.V-{s})
        u.color=WHITE
        u.d=inf
        u.parent=nil
    
    s.color=GRAY
    s.d=0
    s.parent=nil
    ENQUEUE(Q, s)

    while Q is not empty:
        u=DEQUEUE(Q)
        for each v in G.Adj(u)
            if v.color=WHITE
                v.color=GRAY
                v.d=u.d+1
                v.parent=u
                ENQUEUE(Q, v)
        u.color=BLACK
